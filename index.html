Our Essential Features

Email Integration

Easily share real-time student progress, achievements, and important updates directly to parents' inboxes. Streamline the flow of information, fostering a collaborative educational environment where everyone is informed and engaged.

Easy Attendance Management

Experience effortless attendance management with Al-Hisab, revolutionizing the way schools track and monitor student presence. Our user-friendly interface allows educators to efficiently record and access attendance data in real-time, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information.
